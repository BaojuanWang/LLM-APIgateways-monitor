What Data We Collect We collect only the data necessary to provide and improve our service: Account information: email address, display name, and account password (hashed and salted — we never store plain-text passwords). Payment records: transaction IDs, amounts, and dates from NOWPayments. We do not store cryptocurrency wallet addresses or any sensitive financial details. Technical data: IP address, browser/user-agent string, and session information when you use the web interface. 3. What We Do NOT Collect We do not read, store, or log the content of your API requests (prompts) or the responses from the model providers. The content you send through the API passes through our system briefly for routing, but is not retained. We do not track your activity across other websites, sell your data, or use third-party advertising trackers. 4. How We Use Your Data We use your data for the following purposes: To create and manage your account. To process payments and track your credit balance. To operate, monitor, and troubleshoot the API service. To communicate with you about your account, billing, or service changes. To improve the platform&#x27;s performance, reliability, and user experience. We do not use your data for automated decision-making or profiling. 5. Data Storage &amp; Security All data is stored on servers hosted by Hetzner in Germany. We use industry-standard encryption (TLS) for data in transit and encryption at rest for sensitive data. Passwords are hashed using a strong, salted algorithm. While we take reasonable measures to protect your data, no internet-based service can guarantee absolute security. We encourage you to use strong, unique passwords and keep your API keys confidential. 6. Cookies We use minimal cookies and local storage only for essential functionality: session management (keeping you logged in), and remembering your theme preference (light/dark mode). Their privacy policy applies to that interaction. Legal obligations: If required by law, court order, or valid legal process, we may disclose data to authorities. Service providers: We use Hetzner (hosting) and potentially email delivery services. These providers process data only on our instructions and are contractually bound to protect it. 8. Data Retention We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. API usage logs are retained for up to 12 months for billing and debugging purposes, then anonymized or deleted.
